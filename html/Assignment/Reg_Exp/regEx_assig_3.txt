The ghost of match fixing has returned to haunt world cricket with British tabloid The News of the World revealing that Indian cricketers were allegedly involved in deliberately underperforming in the Lord's Test that England won by an innings. Based on a sting operation conducted by The News of the World, the Metropolitan Police arrested London-based property tycoon Mazhar Majeed, who allegedly lured Indian fast bowlers Munnaf Patel and Zaheer Khan to deliver three blatant no-balls at the agreed moment of the game by the daily's undercover reporter
